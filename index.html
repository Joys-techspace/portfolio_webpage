About Joy

From legal eagle to data diva, my professional journey has taken me on a fascinating ride. As a Civil/Business Lawyer, I honed my legal and analytical skills to provide effective solutions for my clients. But after six years, I yearned for a new challenge. Enter the world of banking, where I served as a Legal Analyst for a commercial bank..

But that wasn't enough. I wanted to explore the limitless potential of data to inform decision-making. So I took the leap and transitioned into a career in data analysis, leveraging my legal training to uncover insights and drive results. Join me on my journey as I explore the fascinating intersection of law, data, and analytics.

Discovering my passion for data analysis wasn't easy, but I was determined to make a career transition. Armed with grit and a thirst for knowledge, I enrolled in bootcamps and delved into the world of programming languages, statistical analysis, and data visualization. Combining my legal and business acumen, I focused on projects that centered on data analysis in the business and retail sector. With each project, I honed my skills and built an impressive portfolio that opened doors to exciting opportunities. Today, I'm proud to have successfully made the leap from law to data analysis and excited to explore this field's limitless potential.

Udemy Sales Dashboard

View Interactive Dashboard
View Report

The online learning platform Udemy offers a wide range of courses across four major subjects, which are available at various levels, including free and paid options. However, there is a need to understand the sales performance of specific courses and the key factors that affect their popularity to maintain the company's growth.

This project aims to analyze the sales performance of four Udemy courses using a comprehensive dataset that includes information about course prices, durations, and number of subscribers. By identifying the critical factors affecting sales and popularity, this analysis provides valuable insights into potential strategies that the company could implement to improve course offerings and sustain growth.
